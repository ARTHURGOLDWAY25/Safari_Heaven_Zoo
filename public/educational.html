where learning rules and curiosity is cool

At Safari Heaven Zoo, we believe that education should be an adventure, sparking curiosity and inspiring a lifelong love of learning. Our programs are designed to engage minds of all ages, fostering a deep connection with the natural world and its inhabitants. Join us in exploring the wonders of wildlife through hands-on experiences, interactive exhibits, and captivating stories that make learning both fun and impactful. Discover a place where every question leads to an exciting journey, and curiosity knows no bounds.

for schools

Plan a Field Trip

Plan an unforgettable field trip to Safari Heaven Zoo, where students can immerse themselves in the wonders of wildlife. Our field trips offer hands-on learning experiences through interactive exhibits, guided tours, and up-close animal encounters. Perfect for schools and educational groups, these trips spark curiosity, inspire a love for nature, and provide valuable insights into animal care and conservation. Join us for a day of discovery and adventure, bringing education to life beyond the classroom.

Zoo Explorers

Join the Zoo Explorers program at Safari Heaven Zoo, where young adventurers embark on exciting journeys through the animal kingdom. This program offers engaging activities, interactive learning, and behind-the-scenes tours, allowing children to discover the fascinating world of wildlife. Designed to ignite curiosity and foster a love for nature, Zoo Explorers provides unforgettable experiences that inspire and educate, making each visit a memorable adventure.

Zoo Investigations

Dive deep into the mysteries of the animal kingdom with our Zoo Investigations program. Designed for curious minds, this program offers hands-on experiences, interactive exhibits, and in-depth explorations of wildlife and their habitats. Participants will engage in scientific investigations, uncover fascinating animal behaviors, and learn about the critical role of conservation. Zoo Investigations provides a unique opportunity to explore the wonders of nature, inspiring a deeper understanding and appreciation of the natural world.
